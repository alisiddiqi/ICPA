Who we are

Our goal is to be the centeral Competitive programming organization in Canada

ICPA is a not-for-profit, which is run by students for students. We are a decentralized organization that allow competitive programmers from around the world to come together and learn from each other.

Our future vision is to expand our organization to every major university in the province of Alberta. By having a lot of different universities, ICPA will have leverage when negotiating with sponsorships. Sponsorships will be used to gain funding for programming competitions in Alberta.


Objectives

Allow Competetive Programmers from around the globe to communicate with each other

Create a central organization for hosting events

Create a strong coalition to make it easier to gain financial backing

Helpers in the creation of programming clubs
